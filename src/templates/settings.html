What's New

            

            

        

    


    
    

        

            
            
Reboot device?

            

            
This will interrupt the display and make the web UI unavailable for approximately 30 seconds. If the device fails to come back online, physical access will be required to recover.

            

                
                
            

        

    


    
    

        

            
            
Roll back update?

            

            
This will check out the previous version (—) and re-run the installer. The UI may be briefly unavailable while the service restarts.

            

                
                
            

        

    


    
    

        

            
            
Shut down device?

            

            
This will power off the device. The web UI will remain unavailable until the device is manually restarted. Physical access is required to turn it back on.
